About Us

Visual language Interpreter

Communication is a fundamental aspect of human interaction, yet millions of people around the world face barriers due to speech and hearing impairments. Traditional sign language serves as a vital means of communication for the deaf and Mute, but it often limits their ability to interact with those who do not understand it. To bridge this gap, "Visual language Interpreter" seeks to provide an innovative solution that translates sign language into audible speech, facilitating seamless communication between sign language users and the broader community.
